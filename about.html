The Problem We're Solving

Cloud costs are the fastest-growing line item in most technology budgets — and the least understood. Traditional FinOps tools give you dashboards full of numbers but leave the hard work to you: figure out what's wrong, work out how to fix it, write the Terraform, get it approved, apply it. By the time that cycle completes, the problem has compounded.

The enterprise tools that do offer automation cost £50,000+ per year and are built for Fortune 500 procurement cycles, not the engineering teams who actually manage infrastructure. Smaller teams are left with spreadsheets and gut feel.

FinOpsMind closes that gap. ML-powered detection finds the problems. Intelligent rules generate the fix. An agentic AI layer applies what it can autonomously and routes everything else through your approval model. You stay in control without being in the way.

How We Built It

FinOpsMind is built from first principles — no off-the-shelf ML frameworks, no third-party cost APIs acting as intermediaries. Direct integration with AWS Cost Explorer, Azure Cost Management, and GCP Cloud Billing means we see your costs the same way your cloud provider does.

The ML pipeline runs Isolation Forest and Random Cut Forest anomaly detection alongside Prophet-based time series forecasting. The recommendation engine applies 60+ rules derived from years of real-world cloud architecture experience across banking, manufacturing, and enterprise SaaS.

The v2 agentic layer — currently in development — adds three specialised AI agents that reason about your infrastructure, plan remediation actions, and execute them autonomously within the permission boundaries you define.

Where We're Headed

FinOpsMind v1 is in active testing with early customers — a full-stack platform covering anomaly detection, forecasting, and a 60+ rule recommendation engine with Terraform generation.

v2 introduces the Agentic AI layer: three specialised agents — Discovery, Optimisation, and Governance — that operate continuously against your infrastructure. The Discovery Agent maps your resources in real time. The Optimisation Agent applies intelligence rules and generates remediation plans. The Governance Agent enforces your permission model, routes actions by risk tier, and maintains a full audit trail.

The result is a system that doesn't just find waste — it eliminates it, within the boundaries you control, with full visibility into every action taken.
